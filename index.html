Technical SEO

The Engine
Under the Hood

Rankings are built on solid technical foundations. I audit, fix, and future-proof websites to meet what search engines actually reward.

🔬

SEO Audits

Comprehensive crawl-based audits identifying indexing issues, redirect chains, duplicate content, broken links, and structural problems that silently kill rankings.

🏗️

Schema Markup

Implementing structured data (Article, Product, FAQ, Breadcrumb, Organization) to unlock rich results, improve SERP features, and build AI-readable trust signals.

⚡

Core Web Vitals

LCP, INP, and CLS optimisation - working with dev teams to pass Google's page experience thresholds and ensure performance metrics don't become a ranking liability.

🕷️

Crawl Optimization

Robots.txt management, XML sitemap optimization, crawl budget allocation, and internal link architecture - ensuring Googlebot discovers and indexes what matters.

📑

Indexation Management

Canonical tags, noindex directives, and faceted navigation control to prevent index bloat and ensure only the right pages compete in search.

🔗

Site Architecture

Internal linking strategy, content siloing, and URL structure planning to distribute PageRank efficiently and signal topical relevance at scale.
